  title: Publications
  description: >
    The Museum takes care of the edition of a scientific journal called "Quaderni del Museo Civico di Nepi" born in 2006. Part of the publication is a series called “Archeologia e Storia a Nepi” ("Archeology and History in Nepi") which collects texts from the conferences organized by the Museum since 2000. The Civic Museum of Nepi produces also scientific, popular and educational publications.
